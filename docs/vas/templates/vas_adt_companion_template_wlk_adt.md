## Section 4 – Core Governance Principles (in brief)

> This section summarises key governance tenets derived from WS-ViC-001 and WS-Framework-Charter. Full conformance is assessed in Section 5a.

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Principle of Truth** | All system outputs must be truthful and verifiable; no deliberate misinformation. |
| **Principle of Justice** | Decisions must be fair, unbiased, and aligned with ethical standards. |
| **Principle of Order** | Systems must operate in a controlled, deterministic, and traceable manner. |
| **Principle of Balance** | Trade-offs (security, privacy, performance) must be documented and justified. |
| **Responsible Care** | Proactive protection of human dignity, safety, and fundamental rights. |
| **Zero Tolerance** | Absolute prohibition of child exploitation content or related activities. |
| **MHO** | Meaningful Human Oversight must be exercised at all critical decision points. |
| **Need-to-Know / Right-to-Know** | Information access and transparency obligations are strictly observed. |
| **Gateway Control** | Data and decisions flow only from high-trust to low-trust layers, not vice versa. |
| **Traceability** | All decisions and changes are logged with full auditability. |

## Annex F – ISO 42001 Mapping & Application Guide (Optional for external parties)

> **Purpose:** This annex provides a structured mapping between **ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (AI Management System)** clauses and the **WS-ViC-001 / WS-Framework-Charter** principles, as operationalised in the VAS ADT Companion.  
> **Status:** *Non‑binding for WS‑internal use. Provided as a translation layer for organisations required to implement ISO 42001.*

| ISO 42001 Clause | Requirement Summary | Corresponding WS Principle / Section | VAS ADT Evidence Required | Critical Note (Orbital Reality) |
|-------------------|----------------------|---------------------------------------|---------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **5.1 (Leadership)** | Top management commitment | Principle of **Responsible Care** + MHO (Section 6) | MHO authority levels & escalation logs | *Leadership must be accountable to Truth, not just budgets.* |
| **6.1 (Actions to address risks)** | AI risk management | Principle of **Balance** + ISO 31000 alignment (Section 9) | Risk register with MHO sign-off | *Risks to human dignity override business risks.* |
| **7.2 (Competence)** | Personnel competence | MHO (Section 6) + Need-to-Know | Training records & MHO certification | *Oversight competence must include ethical reasoning, not just tech skills.* |
| **8.1 (Operational planning)** | AI system lifecycle control | Gateway Control (High‑to‑Low) (Section 5a.2) | Architecture diagrams & data flow audits | *No low‑trust component may influence high‑trust decisions.* |
| **8.3 (AI system impact assessment)** | Impact assessment on individuals/society | Principle of **Justice** + **Truth** (Section 5a.1) | Ma’at alignment report (Section 5) | *Assess against objective truth, not just stakeholder perception.* |
| **9.2 (Internal audit)** | Internal audits | Entire Section 8 (Audit & Monitoring) | Completed Section 5a tables | *Audits must verify immutable principles, not just checklists.* |
| **10.2 (Non-conformity correction)** | Corrective actions | Section 8 (CAP) + MHO escalation | CAP records with MHO approval | *Corrections must restore Order, not just patch symptoms.* |

**Guidance for external users:**  
To use this VAS ADT Companion for ISO 42001 certification:
1. Complete **Sections 1–5a** (core WS principles) as the foundation.
2. Use the mapping above to map each completed WS evidence item to the respective ISO clause.
